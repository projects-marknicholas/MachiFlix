# MachiFlix v1.0.0

First proper release. Nothing fancy, just gets the job done.

## Pages

- Home: Movie categories split into Popular, Top Rated, Trending, On Air, and Airing. That's it for now.
- Search: Search for movies, TV shows, or people
- Library: Your bookmarked stuff lives here. Movies and shows you saved for later.
- TV Shows: Not implemented yet. Placeholder for now.
- Settings: Change your display name, switch between light and dark mode, check app info.

## Install

Download the APK from the releases section below and install it on your Android device.

Make sure you have "Install from unknown sources" enabled in your settings.

## Requirements

- Android device running version 5.0 or higher
- Internet connection for streaming content
